When To Go

Spring/Summer

May-September

If you prefer to be warm, I would recomment going from the months April-September. These is France's warmer season with the two hottest months being July and August. If you are looking to enjoy the beaches and staying warm, this is the time to schedule your trip. The downside of going during this time is that it is usually very crowded due to the warmer weather and summer vacation.

Fall/Winter

October-April

If you would rather have the whole country to yourself and feel a little less touristy, visiting in October-April would be the best months for you. These months definitely do get pretty cold but if you want to avoid the crowds and pay for a cheaper flight, then I would recommend planning your trip for these months.

Where To Go

A brief summary of three of France's finest cities.

Paris

Paris, aka the city of love, is the capital and most popular city in France. Paris is known for it's many monuments and some of the biggest art musuems in the world.

Nice

Nice, France is a very popular vacation spot that is located on the French Riviera. Known for it's beautiful beaches and clear blue waters.

Bordeaux

If you are a wine drinker, then Bordeaux France will be your favorite destination of the trip. Bordeaux is known as the wine capital of France and is filled with a tremendous amount of winerys.
